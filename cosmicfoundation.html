Cosmic Foundation

At Cosmic Foundation, our mission is to make a profound impact by supporting those in need. We are dedicated to alleviating poverty, combating climate change through reforestation, and providing essential support to orphans. Every prize and service offered by CosmiCode plays a crucial role in advancing our foundation's efforts. All revenue generated from our paid services is mostly reinvested into the foundation's initiatives, ensuring that every resource directly contributes to our noble causes.

"The best of people are those who bring the most benefit to the rest of mankind"
(Hadith)

Cosmic Foundation operates independently without external funding, relying solely on the dedication and efforts of CosmiCode students to drive its mission forward.

Welfare Support

  • At Cosmic Foundation, we provide essential support such as food, clothing, and medical care to improve daily lives and offer aid to those in need.

Green Mission

  • Cosmic Foundation is actively planting trees, with over 5,000 already in the ground, and the effort continues to promote a healthier planet.

Orphan Support

  • Cosmic Foundation helps orphans with education and support, ensuring they have the resources they need for a better future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Cosmic Foundation

  • What is the mission of the Cosmic Foundation?

    The Cosmic Foundation is dedicated to creating a positive and lasting impact through three primary areas: supporting the less fortunate, combating climate change, and supporting orphans. Our mission focuses on alleviating poverty, protecting the environment, and providing care and education to orphan.

  • How does the Cosmic Foundation support the less fortunate?

    We provide essential resources such as food, clothing, and medical care to individuals in need. Our aim is to enhance their daily lives and offer support during challenging times, ensuring they have the basics required to thrive.

  • What initiatives does the Cosmic Foundation have for combating climate change?

    Our reforestation efforts are a key part of our commitment to environmental sustainability. We have planted over 5,000 trees to help restore and protect our environment, contributing to a healthier planet for future generations.

  • Where can we see Cosmic Foundation’s work?

    The work of the Cosmic Foundation will be showcased soon through our official social media channels. Stay tuned for updates on our initiatives and impact. The Cosmic Foundation's YouTube channel is set to go live soon. Stay connected for insightful content and updates on our initiatives.

  • What motivates the Cosmic Foundation’s work?

    Our work is inspired by the Islamic principle, "The best of people are those who bring the most benefit to the rest of mankind" (Hadith). This principle guides our efforts and drives us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we support.

  • How can I get involved with the Cosmic Foundation?

    At Cosmic Foundation, we do not accept donations as all funding and resources for our initiatives come from CosmiCode's paid services. Our dedicated team, alongside a passionate group of students, works tirelessly to achieve our noble causes.

    If you wish to get involved, here are a few ways to contribute:

    • Volunteer: Join our team of volunteers to actively participate in our projects and initiatives. Your time and skills can make a significant impact.
    • Participate in CosmiCode’s Programs: Engage in the various paid services and programs offered by CosmiCode. Your participation directly supports the foundation’s efforts.
    • Spread the Word: Help us raise awareness about our mission and initiatives by sharing our work within your network and community.
